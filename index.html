Care tips
🧽
Clean after wet conditions
Mud and salt accelerate sole degradation. Soft brush + warm water within 24hrs.
🌬️
Air dry only — never tumble dry
Heat warps the Air unit. Stuff with newspaper to retain shape while drying.
📦
Store away from direct sunlight
UV causes yellowing. A shoe box or dark shelf extends life by 1–2 years.
