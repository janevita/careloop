How it works

Three steps to consuming consciously.

No research. No greenwashing. Just honest scores and better choices.

1
Scan anything
Point your camera at a barcode, photograph a label, or just ask a question. Care Loop understands any product in seconds.
→
2
Get the score
Your Conscious Index breaks the product down across Health, Planet, Ethics, and Longevity — with full transparency on how we decided, and what we couldn't verify.
→
3
Act on it
Compare honest alternatives, add the product to your care log, get tips to make it last longer, and find the right way to repair or responsibly let it go.
What you get

Everything you need to consume with intention.

🔍
The Conscious Index
Four scores — Health, Planet, Ethics, Longevity — unified in one view, with clear reasoning and flagged unknowns. We never hide what we don't know.
🔄
Conscious Alternatives
Every product comes with better options across a range of prices. Compare trade-offs side by side — not just rankings, but reasons.
🛠
Care & Longevity Hub
Personalised care guides, repair pathways, and maintenance reminders for everything you own. The app that helps you use things fully.
♻️
Responsible Disposal
When you're done, we find the best outcome: repair, sell, donate, recycle — in that order. Location-aware, step-by-step guidance.
🤝
Community Wisdom
Real reviews structured around ownership, not just satisfaction. Local repair events and crowdsourced greenwashing alerts.
🌿
My Impact
A personal log of every conscious choice — products owned, CO₂ saved, money not wasted on premature replacements. Progress you can see.
